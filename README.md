# Cyber-Rush

🚀 Game Description

This is a fast-paced 3D endless runner set in a vibrant cyberpunk world. Dash along a glowing neon highway, dodge dangerous obstacles, collect powerful upgrades, and push your reflexes to the limit. As your speed increases, the challenge intensifies—how long can you survive?

✨ Gameplay Highlights

3 Lanes – Switch left or right to dodge obstacles
Jump & Slide – Jump over barriers or slide under hazards
Endless Track – A constantly changing road ensures every run feels unique
Increasing Speed – The longer you survive, the faster it gets
Power-Ups – Collect shields, coin magnets, and score multipliers
Neon Effects – Enjoy glowing visuals, particle trails, and explosions
3 Difficulty Levels – Choose between Easy, Medium, or Hard
Score & Coins – Aim for high scores and collect as many coins as possible
Jump Safety – Brief invincibility when you jump helps avoid tight collisions
